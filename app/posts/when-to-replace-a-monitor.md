A monitor does not have to fail in a dramatic way for it to become a poor choice. It can simply become too limited for the way you use it. If the screen is dim, slow, or uncomfortable to look at for long periods, the experience can degrade even if the PC itself is still strong.

Common signs include poor contrast, weak color accuracy, uncomfortable brightness, and refresh rates that feel outdated for modern gaming or creative work. If you have been compensating for a weak display for a while, the upgrade may bring more value than you expect.

The right time to replace a monitor is when it stops supporting your workflow comfortably. That can happen for work, gaming, media, or just general daily use. A good display improves everything else around it.
